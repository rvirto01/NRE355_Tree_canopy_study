About the neighborhood

The Ashland neighborhood is a small historical neighborhood in Lexington. We studied the area to be ~163.89 acres with a 19% tree canopy cover using the 2016 NAIP imagery and NDVI analysis. We discovered the tallest trees in the neighborhood to be on the historic Henry Clay estate. They were a 113 ft Spruce, 108 ft Tulip Tree, and 107 ft Tulip Tree.
