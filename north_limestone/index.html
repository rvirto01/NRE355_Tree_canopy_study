About the neighborhood

North Limestone is a larger neighborhood of 579.4 acres. The interesting features include Transylvania University, multiple schools, Doodles Restaurant, Carrick House, and Church on 4th Street. From the 2013 tree canopy assessment North Limestone has 25.5% tree canopy cover. We discovered the tallest tree was 102 ft tall. Using the 2014 NAIP imagery we found the canopy to be 22.7% and in 2016 it was 17.7%. This shows a gradual decrease in canopy cover in the area.
