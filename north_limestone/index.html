North Limestone Neighborhood Association

North Limestone is a larger neighborhood of 579 acres. The interesting features include Transylvania University, multiple schools, Doodles Restaurant, Third Street Stuff Coffee, and a historic church on 4th Street. From the 2013 tree canopy assessment North Limestone has 25.5% tree canopy cover. We discovered the tallest tree was 102 ft tall and on private property.
