Cardinal Valley Neighborhood Association

Cardinal Valley is a 336 acre suburban neighborhood located in northwestern Lexington. It features very interesting points of interest including an elementary school and many residential homes. This neighborhood is known for a Latino influence, which can be seen in the Spanish names of many of the nearby businesses and churches. It's tree canopy coverage is 32.4%.
