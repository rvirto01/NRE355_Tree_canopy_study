About the neighborhood

Cardinal Valley is a 335.89 acre suburban neighborhood located in northwestern Lexington. It features very interesting points of interest including an elementary school and many residential homes. This neighborhood is known for a Latino influence, which can be seen in the Spanish names of many of the nearby businesses and churches. In 2014, tree canopy covered 26% of Cardinal Valley. This decreased to 14.5% in 2016.
