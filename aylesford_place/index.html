About the neighborhood

Aylesford Place is a 244.69 acre neighborhood located close to Downtown Lexington. Notable features include places such as Woodland Park and Common Grounds CoffeeHouse. We discovered the tallest tree was a 96 ft Gingko Tree. The percent canopy cover was 26.98% in 2014 and in 2016 it was 22.41%.
