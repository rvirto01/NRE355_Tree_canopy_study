Aylesford Place Neighborhood Association

Aylesford Place is a 245 acre neighborhood located close to Downtown Lexington. Notable features include places such as Woodland Park and Common Grounds Coffee House. We discovered the tallest tree was a 110-ft Sycamore. The percent tree canopy cover was 35.9%.
